Development Engineer

Development Engineers are those tasked with overseeing every little aspect of the project, start to finish. They forge the software, find and recieve it's issues, and fix them with resourceful solutions. They're the beating heart of ISP, nothing would get done without them.

QA Engineer

Our QA Engineers take a hammer to our delicately assembled software. They subject our ISP to rigorous testing to ensure it's functionality. Nothing is ready for public use until it's been built, tested, broken down to square one, rebuilt back up, and broken down again. Without QA Engineers, our project would be buggy and experimental, filled with oversights and aberrations.

Project Manager

Our Project Manager is in charge of keeping everyone in line. Efficency is key to success, and efficency is built upon good leadership. The Project Manager will ensure things are running smoothly, and more than sufficient progres is being made to reach our goals in time.

Product Manager

The Product Manager, idealistic but pragmatic. No other role would have a job without a vision and a plan, and our Product Manager delivers. They understand the customer's needs and issues, and use these to set the project's goals. The Product Manager gives our ISP value and meaning.
